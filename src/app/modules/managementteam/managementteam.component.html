MANAGEMENT TEAM


Chief Executive Officer
Ernest Brown

PROFILE

Ernest Brown leads Technology Advantage Group as the Chief Executive Officer with exceptional experience in the Digital Economy Landscape. He is a seasoned Information Technology Entrepreneur with over 20 years’ experience in the Digital Landscape.

In his previous role, he was the C.E.O of Broadband Home Limited, Internet and Data Communications Service provider for 10 years.

Based on his experience in the Digital Space in Ghana, he has served on various boards and Institutions in the Communications Sector of Ghana before becoming the Chief Executive Officer of Technology Advantage Group and has held senior positions in his line of expertise.

He served as a Board member of Ghana Investment Fund for Electronic Communications (GIFEC), an agency under the Ministry of Communication, Board member of National Information Technology Agency(NITA) as Chairman of Technical sub-committee of the board. He was also the President of Internet Service Providers Association for 6 years. Ernest Brown also chaired a Committee of Standardization for IT and Technology Services for Ghana Standards Authority.

Furthermore, he was the Convener of Ghana Network Operators Group (GHNOG) which is an Association of Network Operators – both Private and Government where he led a team of young network engineers to promote proper organizational network infrastructure.

He also managed and consulted for various local and international organizations, in the area of ICT, including Consulting for some Oil Companies in the establishment of a Tier 3 Data Centre and Visualization and Operations Control Rooms.

As part of his efforts in the Digital Space, Technology Advantage Group was adjudged the Emerging ICT Company of the Year and Emerging ICT Consulting Company of the Year 2020 and 2021 during the Ghana Information Technology and Telecoms Awards respectively.

Ernest Brown holds Computer Science and statistics degree from the University of Ghana, a Certificate in Distributed Systems from the International Centre for Theoretical Physics (ICTP) from Trieste, Italy, and a degree in Business in Emerging Markets from the University of Liverpool.

Legal Officer
Emmanuel Amissah

PROFILE

Emmanuel Amissah is a highly qualified and deeply experienced law professional. He has over 20 years working experience as a legal practitioner, having been called to the Ghana Bar in October 1995.

Emmanuel worked as an Associate with Kwaku Kyeremateng & Company, and as Senior Associate at Ghartey & Ghartey. He joined AB & David (formerly General Law Consult) as a Senior Associate, and rose to become a partner. He headed the Commercial Litigation & ADR, as well as the Property & Real Estate practice groups of the firm.

Emmanuel also had a stint at Scancom Limited (operators of MTN Ghana) as the Senior Manager (Site Acquisitions) from September 2008 to February 2010, and has been the in- house counsel for Facol Roads Limited (a road construction & furnishing company) since 1997, until he joined CDH Financial Holdings Ltd.

Emmanuel joined TAG as its Legal Officer in 2020 and is currently a Director/Secretary of Technology Advantage Group.
