TECHNICAL SERVICES

ICT HARDWARE & SOFTWARE SOLUTIONS

Information technology (IT) technical services are critical in modern-day business operations. Every business, no matter its size or domain, relies on IT to operate efficiently and effectively. IT technical services are essential to ensure that our Customers IT infrastructure, applications, and systems are reliable, secure, and performing optimally. By utilizing our different types of IT technical services, our customers are able to minimize the occurrence of technical glitches, downtime, and data security breaches.

We offer a wide range of Technical services including:

In today's fast-paced business world, organizations require an efficient and reliable network infrastructure to support seamless communication, collaboration, and data sharing.
Our Network reengineering and administration product involve
• Designing, • Implementing and • managing network infrastructure to ensure optimum performance, availability, and security.
TAG’s Effective network reengineering and administration rely on several key principles. These include
• Scalability: A scalable network should be designed to support increased traffic, users, and devices without compromising network performance or security
• Availability: Availability refers to the network's ability to provide uninterrupted service to users. A highly available network should be designed with redundant components, backup systems, and disaster recovery plans to minimize downtime.
• Security: Security refers to the network's ability to protect against unauthorized access and data breaches. A secure network should have robust authentication, authorization, and encryption policies to safeguard sensitive information.
• Performance: performance refers to the network's ability to deliver consistent and predictable speed and quality of service (QoS) to users. By following best practices and principles, our customers are able to ensure that their network infrastructure is highly scalable, available, secure, and performs at optimal levels, enabling them to meet their business objectives effectively.
ICT hardware supplies are an essential component of any organization's IT infrastructure. They are the physical devices, machines, and equipment that enable the processing and storage of digital data, as well as the transfer of information across networks. Our ICT hardware services are broken into five (5) distinct areas:

  • Computers and Accessories
  • Servers
  • Storage Devices
  • Backup Power
  • Networking Equipment
  • Other Specialized Technology Equipment

Software implementations and integration are crucial components of any technology-driven organization. Both concepts aim to enhance our customers efficiency by optimizing software usage, thereby improving connectivity and productivity. Software implementation involves the process of selecting, installing, and integrating software applications into an organization's environment, while software integration refers to connecting isolated software systems to achieve a unified and automated experience.

Implementing and integrating software systems is an integral part of an organization's digital transformation. Proper software integration results in increased productivity, improved communication, and streamlined business processes. Therefore, it is paramount for TAG to ensure that our customers select the right software packages, properly configure and install the software, and integrate them efficiently with existing systems.

At TAG we ensure the above to help our customers gain a competitive advantage while achieving their business objectives.
